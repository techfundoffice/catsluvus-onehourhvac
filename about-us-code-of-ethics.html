Our Code of Ethics

The principles that guide everything we do at Cats Luv Us Boarding Hotel.

Our Commitment to Ethical Cat Care

At Cats Luv Us Boarding Hotel, our code of ethics is more than a document — it’s the foundation of every interaction we have with cats and their owners. Since 1993, we have held ourselves to the highest standards of professionalism, compassion, and integrity in everything we do.

We believe that every cat deserves to be treated with dignity and respect, regardless of their breed, age, temperament, or health status. This belief shapes our hiring practices, our training programs, our facility design, and our daily operations.

Animal Welfare Above All

The well-being of every cat in our care is our top priority. We commit to providing clean, safe, and stimulating environments. Our facility meets or exceeds all state and local regulations for animal boarding. We maintain strict hygiene protocols including daily cleaning and sanitization of all boarding suites, grooming stations, and common areas. Every cat receives fresh food, clean water, and a comfortable resting area throughout their stay.

We never use force, punishment, or fear-based handling techniques. Our cat care specialists are trained in low-stress handling methods that minimize anxiety and promote trust. If a cat shows signs of distress, we have established protocols to address the situation immediately and compassionately.

Honesty & Transparency

We believe that trust is earned through honest, open communication. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees or surprise charges. If there are any changes to your cat’s health or behavior during their stay, we contact you immediately. We maintain detailed care logs for every guest and make them available to owners upon request.

Our marketing and promotional materials accurately represent our services, facilities, and capabilities. We never make promises we can’t keep, and we always set realistic expectations with pet parents.

Professional Excellence

All Cats Luv Us team members undergo comprehensive training in feline behavior, health, nutrition, and emergency procedures before they interact with any guest. We require ongoing education and regularly bring in veterinary professionals and feline behavior consultants to keep our team’s skills sharp.

We maintain professional relationships with local veterinary clinics to ensure rapid access to medical care if needed. Our emergency protocols are rehearsed regularly, and we maintain current first-aid certifications for all staff members.

Respect for Pet Parents

We treat every cat owner with the same respect and courtesy we show to their feline family members. We listen carefully to your instructions, preferences, and concerns. We understand that leaving your cat with someone else can be stressful, and we do everything we can to provide reassurance and peace of mind.

We respect your time by maintaining efficient check-in and check-out procedures. We respect your privacy by safeguarding your personal information. And we respect your relationship with your cat by providing updates and photos during extended stays.

Environmental Responsibility

We strive to minimize our environmental impact through responsible waste management, energy-efficient facility operations, and the use of eco-friendly cleaning products that are safe for cats. We recycle where possible and choose sustainable suppliers whenever quality allows.

Community Engagement

Cats Luv Us is committed to being a positive force in the Laguna Niguel community and the broader Orange County area. We actively support local cat rescue organizations, participate in TNR (Trap-Neuter-Return) programs, and offer discounted boarding rates for foster organizations. We believe that every cat deserves a loving home, and we do our part to make that a reality.
