How the Loyalty Program Works

Joining the Cats Luv Us Club is simple. Choose your membership tier, sign up at the front desk or through our contact form, and start enjoying your benefits immediately. Memberships run for 12 months from the date of enrollment and can be renewed at any time.

Premium and VIP members also earn loyalty points with every dollar spent on boarding, grooming, and daycare services. Accumulate enough points and you’ll unlock free stays, complimentary grooming sessions, and exclusive seasonal promotions available only to club members.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my membership mid-year?

Absolutely! You can upgrade to a higher tier at any time. We’ll prorate the difference based on your remaining membership term and apply any unused benefits to your new tier.

Do membership discounts apply to all services?

Yes, your membership discount applies to boarding, grooming, and daycare services. Some exclusions may apply to promotional pricing or specially discounted packages already in effect.

Can I use my membership for multiple cats?

Your membership covers all cats in your household. There is no per-cat charge — simply list all your cats when you enroll and they’ll all enjoy the benefits.

What happens if I don’t renew?

Your benefits remain active until your membership expiration date. After that, any unused benefits (free grooming sessions, upgrade credits) will expire. Loyalty points remain in your account for 90 days after expiration, giving you time to renew without losing them.
