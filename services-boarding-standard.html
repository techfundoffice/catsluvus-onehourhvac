Standard Boarding Suite

Our Standard Suite is the perfect choice for cats who want a cozy, comfortable, and secure home away from home. Each standard suite is a private, enclosed space where your cat can relax, play, and rest without disturbance from other guests. Designed with feline comfort in mind, these suites provide everything a cat needs for a happy stay.

Suite Features

  • Approximately 4’ x 3’ private suite with solid walls for privacy
  • Soft, washable bedding replaced daily
  • Individual scratching post
  • Fresh litter box cleaned at least twice daily
  • Shelf or platform for lounging and observing
  • Ventilated design with climate control at 68–74°F

What’s Included

Every standard boarding stay includes premium dry and wet cat food served twice daily (or your own food if preferred), unlimited fresh water, daily litter service, daily enrichment play sessions with our cat care specialists, and a daily wellness check. We also provide a complimentary “welcome treat” at check-in to help your cat acclimate to their new surroundings.

Our standard suites are ideal for cats who are easygoing, adaptable, and comfortable in moderately sized spaces. If your cat prefers more room to roam or enjoys window views, consider upgrading to our Luxury Suite.

Pricing

Standard Suite boarding is priced per night. Multi-cat discounts are available for families boarding two or more cats. Please contact us or visit our booking page for current rates and availability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can two cats share a standard suite?

Yes! If your cats are bonded and comfortable together, they can share a standard suite. We offer a discounted rate for the second cat. Our suites are spacious enough for two cats who enjoy each other’s company.

Can I bring my cat’s own food?

Absolutely. If your cat has specific dietary needs or is a picky eater, we encourage you to bring their regular food. This helps maintain consistency and reduces digestive upset during their stay.

What if my cat needs medication?

Our trained staff can administer oral, topical, and injectable medications on your vet’s prescribed schedule. There is no additional charge for standard oral or topical medications for Premium and VIP club members. Non-members pay a small daily medication administration fee.
