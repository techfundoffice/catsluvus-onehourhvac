ESTABLISHED 1993

A Legacy of Feline Care

Cats Luv Us Boarding Hotel was founded in 1993 with a simple but powerful vision: to create a boarding facility designed exclusively for cats. While most pet care businesses cater to dogs first and cats second, our founders recognized that cats have unique needs that deserve specialized attention.

Located at 27601 Forbes Rd in Laguna Niguel, our facility was purpose-built to provide a calm, dog-free environment where cats can feel safe, comfortable, and at home. Over the past three decades, we’ve cared for tens of thousands of feline guests, earning the trust of cat owners throughout South Orange County.

What started as a small boarding operation has grown into a full-service cat care facility offering boarding, grooming, daycare, and medication administration services. Through it all, our commitment to individualized, compassionate cat care has never wavered.
