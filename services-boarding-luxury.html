Luxury Boarding Suite

For the cat who deserves nothing but the best, our Luxury Suite offers a premium boarding experience with enhanced space, amenities, and personalized attention. These oversized suites are designed for cats who appreciate room to explore, natural light, and elevated resting spots — or for pet parents who simply want to treat their feline companion to the finest accommodations available.

Luxury Suite Features

  • Spacious 6’ x 4’ suite — nearly twice the size of our standard
  • Window perch with natural daylight view
  • Multi-level cat furniture with elevated platforms
  • Premium memory foam bedding
  • Individual scratching post and interactive toys
  • Private litter area separated from sleeping and eating zones
  • Enhanced ventilation with HEPA air filtration

Premium Amenities Included

In addition to all the standard boarding inclusions, luxury suite guests enjoy extended one-on-one play sessions (20 minutes, twice daily), premium wet food options, a dedicated “comfort blanket” that stays with your cat throughout their stay, and a complimentary departure grooming brush-out so your cat looks their best when you pick them up.

Luxury suite guests also receive daily photo or video updates sent directly to your email or phone. We know that being away from your cat can be stressful, and these updates provide peace of mind and a window into your cat’s daily life at Cats Luv Us.

Who Is the Luxury Suite For?

The luxury suite is ideal for cats who are active and enjoy vertical space, cats who are sensitive to confined areas, senior cats who benefit from extra room and softer bedding, cats on extended stays (a week or more), and cat parents who want the highest standard of care and comfort for their feline family member.

Pricing

Luxury Suite Rates

Weekly Rate: 7th night free + complimentary bath

Rates subject to change. Contact us for current pricing and availability.

Upgrade from Standard

Already booked a standard suite? You can upgrade to a luxury suite at any time, subject to availability. Simply call us at (949) 582-1732 or let us know at check-in. The difference in nightly rate will be applied to the remaining nights of your cat’s stay.

If you’re planning an extended trip, consider combining our luxury suite with our long-term boarding program for additional savings on stays of 30 days or more.
