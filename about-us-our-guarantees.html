Our Guarantees

Your cat’s comfort is our promise. Here’s how we back that up.

When you trust Cats Luv Us Boarding Hotel with your feline family member, you deserve more than just words — you deserve guarantees. We’ve been building trust with Orange County cat owners since 1993, and we stand firmly behind every service we provide. Our guarantees aren’t just marketing slogans; they’re commitments that we honor every single day.

💪

Satisfaction Guarantee

Your satisfaction is everything to us. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied with the care your cat received during their stay, contact us within 48 hours and we will work with you to make it right. We may offer a complimentary future stay, a partial refund, or additional services at no charge — whatever it takes to earn back your full confidence. We want every cat parent to feel 100% comfortable trusting us with their furry family member, every single time.

🕒

On-Time Drop-Off & Pick-Up

We respect your schedule. When you book a drop-off or pick-up time, we guarantee that we will be ready and waiting. No long lines, no excessive wait times. Our streamlined check-in process means you can drop off your cat and be on your way in under 10 minutes. If we keep you waiting more than 15 minutes past your scheduled time, your next grooming session is on us. We understand that your time is valuable, especially when you’re headed to the airport or returning from a trip.

🛡

Safety & Security Guarantee

Your cat’s safety is non-negotiable. Our facility is equipped with 24/7 climate control, fire detection and suppression systems, secure entry points, and continuous monitoring. All boarding suites are individually secured with no shared spaces between unfamiliar cats. Our staff is trained in feline first aid and CPR, and we maintain active relationships with local emergency veterinary clinics. In the unlikely event of an emergency, we have protocols in place to provide immediate care and notify you right away.

Transparent Pricing — No Hidden Fees

At Cats Luv Us, the price we quote is the price you pay. Period. Before your cat’s stay begins, we provide a complete, itemized estimate of all charges. There are no hidden fees for food, bedding, or basic care — those are all included in your boarding rate. If additional services like grooming or medication administration are requested, we’ll discuss pricing upfront and get your approval before proceeding.

We believe that financial surprises have no place in pet care. You’re already trusting us with something precious — your cat. The least we can do is be completely upfront about costs. Our pricing is posted on our website and available at the front desk, and our staff is always happy to walk you through a detailed quote.

Our Promise to Your Cat

Beyond our formal guarantees, we make a daily promise to every cat in our care: we will treat them with the same love, patience, and attention that you give them at home. We will learn their preferences, respect their boundaries, and go above and beyond to make sure they feel safe, comfortable, and happy during their stay with us.

That’s the Cats Luv Us guarantee — and it’s been our driving principle for over 30 years.
