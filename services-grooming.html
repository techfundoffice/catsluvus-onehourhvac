Professional Cat Grooming Services

Cats Luv Us Boarding Hotel offers professional grooming services designed specifically for felines. While cats are naturally clean animals, regular grooming by trained specialists helps prevent matting, reduces shedding, controls hairballs, and keeps your cat’s skin and coat in optimal condition. Our experienced groomers understand feline behavior and use gentle, low-stress techniques to ensure a positive experience for every cat.

Whether your cat needs a quick maintenance session or a full spa treatment, we have services to match. All grooming is performed in our dedicated grooming area, separate from boarding suites, using professional-grade tools and cat-safe products.

Why Professional Grooming Matters

Regular professional grooming offers numerous health benefits for your cat beyond just looking good. Matted fur can cause skin irritation, restrict movement, and hide parasites or skin conditions. Overgrown nails can curl into paw pads, causing pain and infection. Excess ear wax can lead to ear infections if not addressed. Our groomers are trained to identify these issues early and address them gently.

For long-haired breeds like Persians, Maine Coons, and Ragdolls, professional grooming is particularly important. These cats are prone to severe matting that can be difficult and painful to remove at home. Regular grooming appointments — typically every 4 to 6 weeks — help prevent mats from forming and keep the coat manageable.

Our Approach to Cat Grooming

We know that many cats are not fans of grooming, which is why our approach prioritizes patience, gentleness, and low-stress handling. Our groomers take the time to let each cat acclimate to the grooming area before beginning. If a cat shows signs of excessive stress, we may split the grooming session across two visits rather than pushing through and creating a negative association.

We use warm water (never cold), cat-specific shampoos and conditioners, and professional dryers set to low heat and gentle airflow. Every tool is sanitized between cats to prevent cross-contamination.

Grooming Add-Ons

  • Flea bath treatment (with cat-safe flea shampoo)
  • De-shedding treatment for heavy shedders
  • Sanitary trim (belly and rear)
  • Lion cut (full body clip with mane and tail pom)
  • Medicated bath (for cats with skin conditions, per vet recommendation)
