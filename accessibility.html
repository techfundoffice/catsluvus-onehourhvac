Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making our website and services accessible to everyone.

Our Commitment

Cats Luv Us Boarding Hotel is committed to ensuring that our website and services are accessible to all individuals, including those with disabilities. We strive to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards to provide an inclusive and welcoming digital experience for all visitors.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Keyboard Navigation: All pages and interactive elements can be navigated using a keyboard alone, without requiring a mouse.
  • Screen Reader Compatibility: Our site is designed to work with popular screen readers, using semantic HTML, ARIA labels, and descriptive alt text for images.
  • Text Resizing: Content scales properly when text size is increased through browser settings, up to 200% without loss of functionality.
  • Color Contrast: We use color combinations that meet WCAG AA contrast ratio requirements to ensure readability for visitors with visual impairments.
  • Descriptive Links: Links use descriptive text that conveys their purpose, rather than generic labels like “click here.”
  • Form Labels: All form fields include clear, visible labels and instructions to assist users in completing forms accurately.
  • Consistent Navigation: Our site uses a consistent navigation structure across all pages to support predictable browsing.

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we are continually working to improve the user experience on our website. We regularly review our site for accessibility issues, test with assistive technologies, and make updates as needed to address barriers.

Physical Accessibility

Our physical facility at 27601 Forbes Rd, Laguna Niguel, CA 92677 includes accessible parking, wheelchair-accessible entrance and reception area, and accessible restrooms. If you need accommodations when visiting our facility, please let us know in advance and we will do our best to assist you.

Third-Party Content

Our website may include content or links to third-party websites that are not under our control. While we encourage these parties to adhere to accessibility standards, we cannot guarantee the accessibility of external content.
