Preparing for Your Cat’s Visit

To make check-in as smooth as possible, please have the following ready before your cat’s first visit:

  • Current vaccination records (FVRCP and rabies)
  • Proof of flea treatment within the past 30 days
  • Any medications in original containers with dosing instructions
  • Your cat’s regular food (if preferred over our premium options)
  • A favorite toy or blanket from home (optional but encouraged)
  • Emergency contact information for you and your veterinarian

If this is your cat’s first stay with us, we recommend scheduling a brief facility tour so both you and your cat can get familiar with the environment. Tours are available during regular business hours and take about 15 minutes.
