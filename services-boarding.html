Cat Boarding

CAT BOARDING

Safe, Comfortable Boarding Designed for Cats

At Cats Luv Us Boarding Hotel, cat boarding is our specialty — and it has been since 1993. Unlike facilities that board both dogs and cats, our entire operation is designed around the unique needs of felines. There is no barking, no canine stress pheromones, and no shared spaces with other species. Your cat will enjoy a peaceful, quiet environment from the moment they arrive until you pick them up.

Every boarding stay includes a private suite, fresh food and water, clean litter changed daily, climate-controlled comfort, and daily enrichment activities supervised by our trained cat care specialists. We offer multiple suite types to accommodate different preferences and budgets, from our well-appointed standard suites to our spacious luxury accommodations.

What’s Included in Every Boarding Stay

  • Private, individually secured boarding suite
  • Premium cat food (or your cat’s own food if preferred)
  • Fresh water refreshed multiple times daily
  • Daily litter box cleaning and sanitization
  • Climate-controlled environment (68–74°F year-round)
  • Daily enrichment and supervised play sessions
  • 24/7 on-site staff presence
  • Health monitoring and daily wellness checks

Boarding Drop-Off & Pick-Up

Our standard drop-off and pick-up hours are Monday through Saturday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Sunday, 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Early drop-off and late pick-up can be arranged in advance for an additional fee. We ask that you schedule your drop-off time when making your reservation to ensure a smooth check-in experience.

During check-in, we’ll review your cat’s feeding schedule, any medications, behavioral notes, and emergency contact information. We want to know everything that will help us make your cat’s stay as comfortable as possible.

Health & Safety Protocols

All boarding guests must be current on FVRCP and rabies vaccinations. We require a flea treatment within the past 30 days. Our facility is cleaned and sanitized daily using cat-safe products, and we maintain strict separation between guests to prevent any spread of illness. Every cat receives a visual health check at drop-off and daily wellness monitoring throughout their stay.
