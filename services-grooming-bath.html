Bath & Brush Service

Our Bath & Brush service is the ideal maintenance grooming option for cats who need a refresh between full grooming sessions, or for cats with shorter coats that don’t require extensive styling. This service cleans, deodorizes, and revitalizes your cat’s coat while also addressing nail and ear care — all in a single, stress-minimized session.

What’s Included

  • Warm water bath with cat-safe, hypoallergenic shampoo
  • Conditioning treatment for coat softness and shine
  • Thorough brush-out to remove loose fur and minor tangles
  • Nail trim on all four paws
  • Ear cleaning and inspection
  • Gentle towel dry and optional low-heat blow dry
  • Light spritz of cat-safe cologne (optional)

Who Is This Service For?

The Bath & Brush is perfect for short-to-medium coat cats who need regular cleaning, cats preparing for a special occasion or photo session, boarding guests who want a fresh-up before pickup, cats who have gotten into something messy at home, and senior cats who have difficulty grooming themselves fully.

This service typically takes 30 to 45 minutes, depending on your cat’s coat type and temperament. Our groomers work at your cat’s pace, ensuring a calm and comfortable experience throughout.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should my cat get a bath?

Most cats benefit from a professional bath every 4 to 8 weeks, depending on their coat type, lifestyle, and any skin conditions. Indoor cats with short coats may need less frequent bathing, while long-haired breeds or cats with oily skin may benefit from more regular sessions.

My cat hates water. Can you still bathe them?

Many cats are uncomfortable with water, and our groomers are experienced in working with reluctant bathers. We use gentle handling techniques, warm water, and a calm environment to make the experience as stress-free as possible. In rare cases where a cat is extremely distressed, we may recommend a waterless shampoo alternative.

Can I combine this with a boarding stay?

Absolutely! Many of our boarding guests add a Bath & Brush to the end of their stay so their cat goes home fresh and clean. Club members receive discounts on grooming services when combined with boarding.
