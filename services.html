FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept dogs?

No. Cats Luv Us is a cat-exclusive facility. We do not board, groom, or provide daycare for dogs. This allows us to maintain a calm, stress-free environment specifically designed for feline guests.

What vaccinations are required?

All cats must be current on FVRCP (feline distemper combo) and rabies vaccinations. We require proof of vaccination from your veterinarian before your cat’s first stay. If your cat is not current, please visit your vet before making a reservation.

Can you administer medication?

Yes! Our trained staff can administer oral, topical, and injectable medications on your vet’s prescribed schedule. Please bring medications in their original containers with clear dosing instructions.

What should I bring for my cat’s stay?

We provide everything your cat needs including food, bedding, litter, and toys. However, you’re welcome to bring your cat’s favorite blanket, toy, or a piece of clothing that smells like home — familiar scents can help cats feel more comfortable in a new environment.

Do you offer emergency or last-minute boarding?

We do our best to accommodate last-minute and emergency boarding requests, subject to availability. We recommend calling us directly at (949) 582-1732 for same-day or next-day boarding needs. Holiday periods book up quickly, so advance reservations are strongly encouraged.
